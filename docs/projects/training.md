# Training & Teaching

![Training](../img/project-9-cover.png){ align=right width="300" .light-bg }

I develop and deliver national training programs to build USGS capacity in non-contact measurement technologies. Teaching and mentoring staff and scientists at all levels is a core part of my mission to operationalize these methods agency-wide.

---

## National Training Courses

### Image Velocimetry Techniques for Computing River Velocity and Discharge

A comprehensive **40-hour national training course** covering the theory, practice, and software tools for image velocimetry. Delivered seven times between 2022–2026 to 170+ USGS staff with 95% satisfaction rates.

**Topics covered:**

- Fundamentals of image velocimetry (STIV, PIV, LSPIV)
- Camera installation and video collection best practices
- IVyTools software operation and workflows
- Orthorectification and ground control
- Quality assurance and uncertainty estimation
- Computing and documenting discharge measurements

### Additional Courses Developed

- **Concepts of Measuring Velocity and Streamflow with Doppler Radar**
- **Using Acoustic Doppler Current Profilers for Velocity and Bathymetry Mapping**
- **Applying Image Velocimetry using Uncrewed Aerial Systems (UAS)**
